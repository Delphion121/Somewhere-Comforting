Photography

Cincinnati based photographer with a passion for shooting portraits. Avantgarde leaning in style, but never one to shy away from natural and simplistic portriats. The desire for portraits comes from the loved ones with a goal to show the person in frame the way I see them in my head: that they are beautiful, and that beauty extends far beyond their physical qualities.

Travel

Twelve countries visited with no plans on stopping - travel has been a crucial part of my adult life. There's still so much untapped potential, even in the places I've visited already. From Germany to Japan to Australia, they all gave me something I'll never forget. I'll never get tired of the modern day advances mixed with decades old cultural standards of Japan - the consistently stellar beaches of Thailand - Australia's diverse and friendly cities. It's always the people, though. The connections have been invaluable and the friendships created under the circumstances of backpacking have brought new meaning to the word 'travel'. The people make the places.

Professional Experience

Work experience is diverse - varied in skillset and work environments. Management and leadership experience, working well with others. Strong focus on building an environment where employees and other coworkers are comfortable under leadership, while still being able to grow and succeed individually. Leadership responsibilities extend beyond the financial aspects of a company - being a trustworthy leader inspires and creates better work circumstances. Strong ability to stay calm and focused in high stress situations regarding customer service, pharmacy technician work, and many other scenarios. Additionally, self sufficient and capable of working alone - multitudes of private contractor/gig work ranging from freelance photographer, copy editor, proofreader, and delivery driver.
